# Learning Activities Legacy Notes

Moved out of the active syllabus on 2026-05-23 during the Fall 2026 cleanup.

## Current decision

- DataCamp remains active, but as certification-based assignments selected according to the course.
- Bonus points apply directly to the next scheduled exam, either a partial exam or the final exam.
- The wheel of fortune remains active as a possible bonus-points activity.
- Stickers, Mentimeter bonus points, DataCamp webinars, videos, mock exams, XP leaderboards, DataCamp assessments, and UN CC:Learn bonus activities are not active in the current syllabus.

## Removed or archived material

### Mock exams

The old syllabus included optional mock exams, sometimes connected to stickers and possible penalties. This is no longer part of the active structure.

### Legacy DataCamp XP system

The old source included a commented system based on accumulating DataCamp XP across the semester, including formulas based on prime numbers and 90,000 XP. This has been replaced by certification-based DataCamp assignments.

### DataCamp assessments and leaderboards

The old source included commented ideas for DataCamp assessments and top-XP leaderboards across 30-, 60-, and 90-day periods. These are not active.

### UN CC:Learn

The old source included commented UN CC:Learn bonus activities. These are not active.

### Stickers

The old visible syllabus included merit stickers worth 5 bonus points each. Stickers are not active.

### Mentimeter

The old visible syllabus included possible Mentimeter bonus points for top answers. Mentimeter bonus points are not active.

### DataCamp webinars

The old visible syllabus included optional DataCamp webinars as non-graded activities. These are not active.

### Videos

The old visible syllabus included individual non-graded videos in Spanish with several possible formats. These are not active.

### Discussion forums

The old source included a commented discussion-forum activity. This is not active.
